The Root Cause

No structured path from symptoms to clinical action. The system is fragmented and reactive — women bounce between GPs, wait months, get dismissed, and research alone.

The Structural Gap

Only 4% of healthcare R&D globally targets women's health. Drug withdrawals are 3.5x more likely due to women's safety risks vs men. Women spend 25% more of their lives in poor health.

Sources: WEF/McKinsey “Closing the Women’s Health Gap” 2024; FDA drug withdrawal analysis 1980–2023
