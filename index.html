Eveline Soares Menezes, PhD

Founder & CEO

Eveline is a Brazilian scientist with a PhD in Kinesiology and Muscle Physiology from Queen's University and over 10 years of research in life sciences. She pivoted from academia to entrepreneurship after witnessing women suffer years of diagnostic delays for conditions like endometriosis and PMOS. Eveline is part of Innovation Factory's Catalyst program and Spring Build, and is currently building Noeva's AI-powered Clarity Engine. She believes clarity is a form of care.

Amanda Della Giustina, PhD

Scientific Advisory Committee

Amanda is a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at the University of Ottawa with over 12 years of preclinical and clinical research experience. Her current work investigates cancer-related muscle loss in ovarian cancer models, while her broader research interest lies in understanding endometriosis as a systemic disease. Her background spans neuroscience, nutrition, and metabolism, bringing a multi-systems perspective to Noeva's clinical pathways. As part of Noeva's Scientific Advisory Committee, Amanda ensures that the AI engine is grounded in the latest evidence.
