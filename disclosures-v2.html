3 AI Technology

AI Infrastructure

Noeva Health's AI-powered health navigation engine is powered by the Anthropic Claude API.

Our AI system analyzes self-reported symptoms against published clinical guidelines to generate personalized care pathway recommendations. The AI does not make autonomous medical decisions; all output is educational and informational in nature.

4 Clinical Evidence Base

Evidence Foundation

Noeva Health's recommendations are grounded in 62+ peer-reviewed citations from leading medical bodies and publications, including:

  • Cochrane -- Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
  • ESHRE -- European Society of Human Reproduction and Embryology
  • SOGC -- Society of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists of Canada
  • NICE -- National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (UK)
  • DSM-5 -- Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders, 5th Edition

Our clinical evidence base is regularly reviewed and updated to reflect the most current published guidelines.

5 Regulatory Status

Classification

Noeva Health is not classified as a Software as Medical Device (SaMD) under Health Canada's regulatory framework.

The Platform is positioned as an educational health navigation tool. It does not diagnose medical conditions, recommend specific treatments, or replace the clinical judgment of licensed healthcare professionals.

For more information, please refer to our Medical Disclaimer.

6 Data Residency

Infrastructure

All personal and health data will be stored on Canadian servers to ensure compliance with PIPEDA and Canadian data residency requirements. Our cloud infrastructure provider and specific hosting region will be confirmed prior to public launch. Data will not leave Canadian jurisdiction. For full details on our data practices, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

7 Privacy Compliance

Regulatory Compliance
